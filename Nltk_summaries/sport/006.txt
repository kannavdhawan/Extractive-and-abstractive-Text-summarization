isinbayeva claims new world best pole vaulter yelena isinbayeva broke her own indoor world record by clearing 4.89 metres in lievin on saturday. in the men s 60m former olympic 100m champion maurice greene could only finish second to leonard scott. it was the russian s 12th world record of her career and came just a few days after she cleared 4.88m at the norwich union grand prix in birmingham. it was greene s second consecutive defeat at the hands of his fellow american who also won in birmingham last week. the olympic champion went on to attempt 5.05m at the meeting on france but failed to clear that height.
