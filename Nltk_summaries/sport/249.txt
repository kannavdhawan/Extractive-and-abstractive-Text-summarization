solskjaer raises hopes of return manchester united striker ole gunnar solskjaer said he hoped to return next season following a career threatening injury to his right knee. solskjaer last played for united as a late substitute in may's fa cup final win over millwall. his contract with united runs out in 2006, by which time he will have been at the premier league club for 10 years. i'm in no doubt that i'll play again, solskjaer told aftenposten daily. the 31 year old norway international had surgery in sweden in august to transplant cartilage into the joint.
