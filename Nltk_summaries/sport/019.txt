london hope over chepkemei london marathon organisers are hoping that banned athlete susan chepkemei will still take part in this year s race on 17 april. the 29 year old has finished second to paula radcliffe in the 2002 and 2003 london races as well as in november s new york city marathon. chepkemei however took part and finished third in last sunday s world best 10k race in puerto rico. chepkemei was suspended from all competition until the end of the year by athletics kenya after failing to report to a national training camp. we are watching it closely said london race director david bedford.
