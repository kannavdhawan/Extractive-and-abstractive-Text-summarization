mirza makes indian tennis history teenager sania mirza completed a superb week at the hyderabad open by becoming the first indian in history to win a wta singles title. in front of a delirious home crowd the 18 year old battled past alyona bondarenko of the ukraine 6 4 5 7 6 3. mirza ranked 134 in the world sunk to her knees in celebration after serving out the match against bondarenko. in a see saw second set bondarenko raced into a 5 2 lead and though mirza hauled herself level the ukrainian broke again before finally levelling the match. at the australian open in january mirza became the first indian woman to reach the third round of a grand slam before losing to eventual champion serena williams. mirza playing in her first wta final began nervously in front of a raucous home crowd committing three double faults in her opening service game.
