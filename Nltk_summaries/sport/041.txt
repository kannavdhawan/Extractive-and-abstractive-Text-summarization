gatlin and hayes win owen awards american olympic stars justin gatlin and joanna hayes have been named the winners of the 2004 jesse owens awards, usa track field's usatf top honour. established in 1981, the jesse owens awards named in honour of the late olympic great who won four gold medals at the 1936 berlin games are presented annually to the outstanding us male and female track and field performers. hayes said knowing some of the great people who have won this award before me and knowing what it represents, i feel a sense accomplishment and pride which lets me know that everything that i have worked for is and always will be worth it. gatlin, 22, won the closest 100m final in games history with a career best time of 9.85 seconds in athens. it is such a great honour to win the jesse owens award, said gatlin.
