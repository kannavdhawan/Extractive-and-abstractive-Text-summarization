o connor aims to grab opportunity johnny o connor is determined to make a big impression when he makes his rbs six nations debut for ireland against scotland on saturday. o connor who celebrated his 25th birthday on wednesday was touted by wasps director of rugby warren gatland as a possible 2005 lions test openside as far back as last september. it looks a definite head to head battle between himself and 23 year old leamy three stone heavier than o connor for the number seven role against the world champions. the wasps flanker replaces denis leamy but o connor knows that the munster man will be pushing hard for a recall for the following game against england. o connor will be winning his third cap after making his debut in the victory over south africa last november.
