navratilova hits out at critics martina navratilova has defended her decision to prolong her tennis career at the age of 48. navratilova who made a comeback after retiring in 1994 will play doubles and mixed doubles events in 2005. women s tennis is really strong she said dismissing suggestions that the fact she could still win reflected badly on the women s game. she will then link up with daniela hantuchova for the australian open doubles and play in the mixed doubles with leander paes. navratilova will partner nathalie dechy in the doubles event at the uncle toby s hardcourts tournament on australia s gold coast which begins on sunday. navratilova has won three grand slam mixed doubles titles since she came out of retirement. and she was so encouraged by her form that she decided to resume playing singles winning two of her seven matches.
