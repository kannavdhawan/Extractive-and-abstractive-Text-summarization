fa decides not to punish mourinho the football association will take no action against chelsea boss jose mourinho following his sending off in sunday s carling cup final. and uefa could also launch disciplinary action following mourinho s failure to attend a compulsory post match press conference after chelsea s champions league defeat at barcelona last week. mourinho still faces an fa investigation into his allegation that manchester united s players cheated during january s carling cup semi final at stamford bridge. in addition some time this month chelsea must also answer a charge of failing to control their players during the premiership win at blackburn in february. and a charge of failing to control their supporters following a carling cup meeting with west ham earlier this season is still to be heard.
