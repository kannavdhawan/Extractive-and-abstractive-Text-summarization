lewis francis eyeing world gold mark lewis francis says his olympic success has made him determined to bag world championship 100m gold in 2005. the 22 year old pipped maurice greene on the last leg of the 4x100m relay in athens to take top honours for team gb. he has confirmed his participation in the norwich union grand prix in birmingham on 18 february, where he will take on another member of britain's victorious men's relay team jason gardener over 60m.
