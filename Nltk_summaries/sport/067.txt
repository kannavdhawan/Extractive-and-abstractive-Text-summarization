lewis francis eyeing world gold mark lewis francis says his olympic success has made him determined to bag world championship 100m gold in 2005. the 22 year old pipped maurice greene on the last leg of the 4x100m relay in athens to take top honours for team gb. he has confirmed his participation in the norwich union grand prix in birmingham on 18 february, where he will take on another member of britain's victorious men's relay team jason gardener over 60m. but individually, the birchfield harrier has yet to build on his world junior championship win four years ago. he added it's a bit too early to make any predictions for helsinki, but i have my eyes open and i know i can be the best in the world. the gold medal in athens has made me realise that i can get to the top level and i want to get there again. lewis francis has still to decided what events will feature in his build up to the worlds with one exception.
