sprinter walker quits athletics former european 200m champion dougie walker is to retire from athletics after a series of six operations left him struggling for fitness. walker had hoped to compete in the new year sprint which is staged at musselburgh racecourse near edinburgh on tuesday and wednesday. the 31 year old scot was suspended for two years in 1998 after testing positive for nandrolone. i missed a big chunk of speed work over about three weeks and then another week working in america. i had intended to race but i m running like a goon said walker.
