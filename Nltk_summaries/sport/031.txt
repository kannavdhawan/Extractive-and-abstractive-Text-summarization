sprinter walker quits athletics former european 200m champion dougie walker is to retire from athletics after a series of six operations left him struggling for fitness. i still enjoy training, but feel it's time to move on, and concentrate on a career. i had intended to race but i'm running like a goon, said walker. walker had hoped to compete in the new year sprint which is staged at musselburgh racecourse near edinburgh on tuesday and wednesday. he told the herald newspaper i'm not in great shape, after missing about a month of training. i missed a big chunk of speed work over about three weeks, and then another week working in america.
