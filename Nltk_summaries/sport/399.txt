farrell saga to drag on lindsay wigan chairman maurice lindsay says he does not expect a quick solution to the on going saga of captain andy farrell s possible switch to rugby union. although the move would be a lucrative one for both farrell and wigan lindsay said money was not a motivating factor for the club. as well as the rugby football union the league the individual club and the england coaching team have a say so it s not a quick decision he said. lindsay also said he understood why rugby union was so interested in signing up farrell. leicester and saracens are leading the chase for the player but lindsay told the bbc it was not yet a done deal.
