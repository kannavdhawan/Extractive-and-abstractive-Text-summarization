mcilroy continues winning streak james mcilroy stormed to his second international victory in less than a week, claiming the men's 800m at the teag indoor meeting in erfurt. the northern ireland runner set a new personal best of one minute, 46.68 seconds a time good enough to qualify for the european indoor championships. the aaa indoor and outdoor champion accelerated away from the field in the final 300m, beating german wolfram mulle by 0.90 seconds. mcilroy is now hoping to gain a late entry into sunday's international indoor meeting in leipzig. on friday, mcilroy delivered an impressive run despite suffering from his bad cold.
