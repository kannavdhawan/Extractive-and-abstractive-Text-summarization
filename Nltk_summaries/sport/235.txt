wenger steps up row arsene wenger has stepped up his feud with sir alex ferguson by claiming the manchester united manager is guilty of bringing football into disrepute. the latest twist in the ferguson wenger saga came on saturday when the united boss in an interview with the independent newspaper discussed the events after the game between the two sides in october. those allegations were put to wenger after saturday s game at bolton which arsenal lost to slip 10 points behind chelsea in the title race. ferguson had also claimed that united chief executive david gill and arsenal vice chairman david dein had agreed at boardroom level not to discuss the incident in public. during that stormy game in october united striker ruud van nistelrooy caught arsenal s ashley cole with one particularly strong tackle.
