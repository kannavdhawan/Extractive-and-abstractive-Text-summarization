ajax refuse to rule out jol move ajax have refused to reveal whether tottenham s boss martin jol is on the dutch champions shortlist to become the amsterdam club s new coach. an ajax spokesman told bbc sport the coach must fit our profile a coach who understands the dutch league and offensive and distinctive football. ajax have enlisted the help of dutch legend johann cruyff currently a consultant at barcelona to help find a new head coach. cruyff has admitted he has been impressed by the way former rfc waalwijk coach jol has turned round spurs fortunes since taking over from jacques santini. jol has been linked with the vacant post at ajax with reports saying he has fallen out with spurs sporting director frank arnesen.
