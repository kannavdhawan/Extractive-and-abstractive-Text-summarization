an integral part of a sustained period of success for england, dallaglio's crowning glory was his part in the side that won the rugby world cup in 2003. rival fans, meanwhile, have tended to take an alternative view, seeing dallaglio as the epitome of the less agreeable characteristics of english rugby. while england did not hit the heights in dallaglio's second spell as captain, losing five of their eight post world cup tests, dallaglio led by example, leaving him as one of the few members of a squad lacking many world cup stars to live up to expectations.
