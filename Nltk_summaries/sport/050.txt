iaaf awaits greek pair's response kostas kenteris and katerina thanou are yet to respond to doping charges from the international association of athletics federations iaaf . the greek pair were charged after missing a series of routine drugs tests in tel aviv, chicago and athens. but kenteris and thanou then went on to skip tests in tel aviv and chicago, when they decided to fly back to greece early. kenteris and thanou already face a criminal trial after being charged with avoiding a drug test on the eve of the athens olympics and then faking a motorcyle crash. the iaaf issued an official warning to the trio last year after they were discovered training in qatar rather than in crete, where they had said they would be.
