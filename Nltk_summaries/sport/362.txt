funding cut hits wales students the wales students rugby side has become a casualty of the welsh rugby union s reorganisation at youth level. players who have played for wales students before going on to win full senior representative honours include robert jones rob howley jon humphreys darren morris martyn williams and ceri sweeney. the secretary of the welsh students rugby football union reverend eldon phillips said it is a shame that fixtures cannot be maintained this year. the increasing number of young rugby players entering higher education look for the biggest challenge that is representative rugby but this year that opportunity will be denied them. the welsh students rugby football union feels that it is unable to properly prepare for or stage the matches.
