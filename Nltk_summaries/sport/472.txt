bryan twins keep us hopes alive the united states kept the davis cup final alive with victory in saturday s doubles rubber leaving spain 2 1 ahead going into the final day. masters cup champions mike and bob bryan thrashed juan carlos ferrero and tommy robredo 6 0 6 3 6 2 in front of a partisan crowd in seville. spain coach jordi arrese chose to rest 18 year old nadal in the doubles after his epic singles win over roddick on friday. when robredo dropped serve in the opening game of the third set the match was all but over and the unflappable bryan brothers powered on to an impressive win. he was replaced by former world number one ferrero but the spanish pair were out of their depth against one of the world s best doubles teams.
