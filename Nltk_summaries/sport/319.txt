pountney handed ban and fine northampton coach budge pountney has been fined 2 000 and banned from match day coaching for six weeks for calling a referee a disgrace . the ban means former scotland international pountney cannot enter the playing enclosure technical areas or go near the touchline tunnel or players and officials areas on the day of a game. pountney was found guilty of bringing the game into disrepute at a rugby football union disciplinary hearing in london on thursday night. pountney pleaded guilty to the offence before a panel consisting of chairman robert horner nigel gillingham and jeff probyn. pountney criticised referee steve lander s performance in northampton s defeat by saracens on 5 february.
