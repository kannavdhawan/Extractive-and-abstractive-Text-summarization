republic to face china and italy the republic of ireland have arranged friendlies against china and italy which will take place at lansdowne road in march and august. the 29 march game will be the second time the republic have played china the previous encounter back in june 1984 with the irish winning 1 0 in sapporo, japan. brian kerr's side will face the 54th ranked chinese on 29 march just three days after the world cup qualifier against israel in tel aviv. ireland round off their world cup campaign with games against france on 7 september, cyprus on 8 october and switzerland on 12 october. we all witnessed the performances of the asian teams in the last world cup, and china play a similar type of football. in their last meeting, the irish beat italy in the 1994 world cup finals.
