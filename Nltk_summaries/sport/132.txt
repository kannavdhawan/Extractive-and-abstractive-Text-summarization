republic to face china and italy the republic of ireland have arranged friendlies against china and italy which will take place at lansdowne road in march and august. brian kerr s side will face the 54th ranked chinese on 29 march just three days after the world cup qualifier against israel in tel aviv. the 29 march game will be the second time the republic have played china the previous encounter back in june 1984 with the irish winning 1 0 in sapporo japan. italy will visit on 17 august in what will be a warm up game ahead of the autumn world cup qualifiers. ireland round off their world cup campaign with games against france on 7 september cyprus on 8 october and switzerland on 12 october.
