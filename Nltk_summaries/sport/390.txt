dawson set for new wasps contract european champions wasps are set to offer matt dawson a new deal. dawson played a vital part in england s world cup win last year but has fallen out of favour with new coach andy robinson after missing a training session in september. the 31 year old world cup winning scrum half has impressed since joining the london side from northampton this summer on a one year contract. wasps coach warren gatland told the daily mirror we have not yet offered matt a new contract but we will be doing so. rugby is still my priority and there s still a burning desire within me to play the best rugby i possibly can he said.
