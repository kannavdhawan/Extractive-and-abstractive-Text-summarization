el guerrouj targets cross country double olympic champion hicham el guerrouj is set to make a rare appearance at the world cross country championships in france. i haven t quite decided what events i will compete in prior to london but the world cross country is an event which is also special to me and is a definite possibility said the two time champion. el guerrouj could meet bekele in march as the ethiopian is the defending world cross country champion over both the long and short courses. and he is aiming to break 10 000m olympic champion kenenisa bekele s 5 000m and 10 000m world records. el guerrouj achieved a lifetime ambition last august when he clinched his first olympic titles over 1500m and 5 000m.
