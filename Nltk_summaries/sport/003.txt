greene sets sights on world title maurice greene aims to wipe out the pain of losing his olympic 100m title in athens by winning a fourth world championship crown this summer. greene was also denied olympic gold in the 4x100m men s relay when he could not catch britain s mark lewis francis on the final leg. greene crossed the line just 0.02 seconds behind gatlin who won in 9.87 seconds in one of the closest and fastest sprints of all time. but greene believes he lost the race and his title in the semi finals. the pair contest the 60m the distance over which greene currently holds the world record of 6.39 seconds.
