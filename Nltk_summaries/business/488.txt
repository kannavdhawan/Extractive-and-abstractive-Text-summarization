us budget deficit to reach 368bn the us budget deficit is set to hit a worse than expected 368bn 197bn this year officials said on tuesday. the budget deficit hit a record 412bn in the 12 months to 30 september 2004 after reaching 377bn in the previous fiscal year. republicans have blamed the size of the deficit on slow economic conditions after the 11 september attacks and ongoing military operations in iraq and afghanistan. but democrats have accused the president of excluding iraq related costs from previous budgets to meet the aim of reducing the deficit a charge which the administration denies. one of president george w bush s election pledges was to halve the budget deficit within five years.
