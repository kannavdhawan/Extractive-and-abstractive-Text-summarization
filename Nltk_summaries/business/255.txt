call to overhaul uk state pension the uk pension system has been branded inadequate and too complex by a leading retirement think tank. the think tank's proposals were made in response to the recent publication of the pensions commission's initial report into uk retirement savings. the report added that a combination of higher taxes, higher savings and or a higher average retirement age was needed to solve the uk pension crisis. the pensions policy institute ppi said replacing the state pension with a citizen's pension would help tackle inequality and complexity. according to the pensions commission's report 12 million working people are not saving enough for their retirement.
