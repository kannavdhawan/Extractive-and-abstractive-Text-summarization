metlife buys up citigroup insurer us banking giant citigroup has sold its travelers life annuity insurance arm to metlife for 11.5bn 6.1bn . robert benmosche metlife s chairman and chief executive said that it was a great opportunity for the brand of metlife to be distributed through citigroup . this deal employs some of metlife s excess capital in a potentially higher return business and gives it more distribution said stuart quint an analyst at gartmore. for metlife the us s leading insurance company the purchase gives it access to a much larger distribution network. the sale is a further move by citigroup away from its 1990s strategy of offering every financial service insurance broking and banking.
