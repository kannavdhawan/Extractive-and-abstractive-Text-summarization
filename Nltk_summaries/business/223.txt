gaming firm to sell uk dog tracks six uk greyhound tracks have been put up for sale by gaming group wembley as part of a move which will lead to the break up of the group. whilst the completion of the sale of the us gaming division remains subject to a number of conditions we believe this development is a positive step towards the maximisation of value for shareholders said wembley chairman claes hultman. us gaming consortium blb will pay 339m 182.5m for the us unit although the deal is subject to certain conditions. wembley announced the planned sale as it revealed it was to offload its us gaming division to blb investors. the sale of the us gaming unit will leave wembley with its uk business.
