in 2003, barclays reported a 20% rise in pre tax profits to 3.8bn, and it has recently forecast similar gains in 2004, predicting that full year pre tax profits would rise 18% to 4.5bn. the two banks reportedly held talks in october and november 2004. barclays shares were up 8 pence, or 1.3%, at 605 pence by late morning in london on monday, making it the second biggest gainer in the ftse 100 index.
