oil companies get russian setback international oil and mining companies have reacted cautiously to russia's decision to bar foreign firms from natural resource tenders in 2005. us oil giant exxon said it did not plan to take part in a new tender on a project for which it had previously signed a preliminary agreement. we regret the ministry has taken such a decision, said ivan kulakov, deputy chairman of highland gold a mining firm that has the motto bringing russia's gold to market .
