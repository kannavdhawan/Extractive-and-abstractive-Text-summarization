dollar hovers around record lows the us dollar hovered close to record lows against the euro on friday as concern grows about the size of the us budget deficit. there are no dollar buyers now, especially after the chicago data yesterday, said abn amro's paul mackel. investors will now look towards february's meeting of finance ministers from the g7 industrialised nations in london for clues as to whether central banks will combine forces to stem the dollar's decline. this has amplified reactions to news, analysts said, adding that they expect markets to become less jumpy in january. the dollar was trading at 1.3652 versus the euro on friday morning after hitting a fresh record low of 1.3667 on thursday. at the same time, german chancellor gerhard schroeder and italian prime minister silvio berlusconi voiced concerns about the strength of the euro.
