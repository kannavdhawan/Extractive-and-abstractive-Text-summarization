burren awarded egyptian contracts british energy firm burren energy has been awarded two potentially lucrative oil exploration contracts in egypt. the firm, which floated in 2003, recently announced a deal to buy 26% of indian firm hindustan oil exploration. the company successfully bid for the two contracts, granted by government owned oil firms, covering onshore and offshore areas in the gulf of suez. this latest contract expands burren energy's global exploration and production portfolio it also holds contracts in turkmenistan and the republic of congo. burren energy already has a presence in egypt, having been awarded an exploration contract last year.
