news corp eyes video games market news corp, the media company controlled by australian billionaire rupert murdoch, is eyeing a move into the video games market. we are struggling with the gap between companies like electronic arts, which comes with a high price tag, and the next tier of companies, he explained during a conference in phoenix, arizona. according to the financial times, chief operating officer peter chernin said that news corp is kicking the tires of pretty much all video games companies . mr chernin, however, told the ft that news corp was finding it difficult to identify a suitable target. the success of products such as sony's playstation, microsoft's x box and nintendo's game cube have boosted demand for video games.
