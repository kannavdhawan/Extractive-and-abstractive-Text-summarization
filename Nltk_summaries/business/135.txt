the commission s controversial 2002 ruling gave protected designation of origin status to feta cheese made in greece effectively restricting the use of the feta name to producers there. feta cheese battle reaches court a row over whether only greece should be allowed to label its cheese feta has reached the european court of justice. denmark is europe s second largest producer of feta after greece producing about 30 000 tonnes a year and exports its products to greece. the original decision was a victory for greece where feta cheese is believed to have been produced for about 6 000 years. from 2007 onwards greek firms will have the exclusive use of the feta label and producers elsewhere in europe must find another name to describe their products.
