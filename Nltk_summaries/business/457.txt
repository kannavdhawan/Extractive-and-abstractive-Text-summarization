germany calls for eu reform german chancellor gerhard schroeder has called for radical reform of the eu s stability pact to grant countries more flexibility over their budget deficit mr schroeder said existing fiscal rules should be loosened to allow countries to run deficits above the current 3 limit if they met certain criteri countries which exceed the threshold are liable to heavy fines by the european commission although several countries including germany have breached the rules consistently since 2002 without facing punishmen mr schroeder a staunch supporter of the pact when it was set up in the 1990s said exemptions were now needed to take into account the cost of domestic reform programmes and changing economic condition
