saab to build cadillacs in sweden general motors, the world's largest car maker, has confirmed that it will build a new medium sized cadillac bls at its loss making saab factory in sweden. beyond the 12,000 job cuts announced last year at its european operations, gm is reducing expenditure by building saabs, opels badged as vauxhalls in the uk and now cadillacs on the same framework, and by allowing the different brands to share parts.
