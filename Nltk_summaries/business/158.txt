qatar and shell in 6bn gas deal shell has signed a 6bn 3.12bn deal with the middle eastern sheikhdom of qatar to supply liquid natural gas lng to north america and europe. shell s agreement under the name qatargas 4 foresees the building of new facilities to handle 1.4 billion cubic feet of gas and 7.8 million tonnes of lng each year from 2011 onwards. france s total is expected to join the exxonmobil scheme dubbed qatargas 2 on monday taking 5 million tonnes of lng a year. exxonmobil will be taking some 15 million tonnes each year for 25 years from the end of 2007 under the deal. the agreement is the latest in a string of deals reached by qatar which is trying to make itself a regional leader in natural gas.
