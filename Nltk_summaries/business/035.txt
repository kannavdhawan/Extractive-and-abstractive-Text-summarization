ad firm wpp's profits surge 15% uk advertising giant wpp has posted larger than expected annual profits and predicted that it will outperform the market in 2005. pre tax profits rose 15% from a year ago to reach 546m 1.04bn , ahead of average analysts' forecasts of 532m. revenues were 4.3bn while the firm's operating margins were 14.1%, which it said could reach 14.8% by 2006. during the year wpp bought us rival grey global, creating a giant big enough to rival sector leader omnicom.
