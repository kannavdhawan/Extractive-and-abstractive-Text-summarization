verizon seals takeover of mci verizon has won a takeover battle for us phone firm mci with a bid worth 6.8bn 3.6bn reports say. mci is the us s second biggest long distance firm after at t. consolidation in the us telecommunications industry has picked up in the past few months as companies look to cut costs and boost client bases. buying mci would give either qwest or verizon access to mci s global network and business based subscribers. the us telecoms market is consolidating fast with former long distance giant at t being bought by former subsidiary sbc earlier this year for 16bn. a merger between mci and verizon would be the fifth billion dollar telecoms deal since october.
