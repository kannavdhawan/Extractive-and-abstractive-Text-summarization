yukos accused of lying to court russian oil firm yukos lied to a us court in an attempt to stop the russian government selling off its key production unit the court has heard. yukos argued that since it had a us subsidiary and local bank accounts the us court could declare it bankrupt and stop the auction of yugansk. its lawyer hugh ray told the court that yukos had claimed it had transferred 27m into two texas bank accounts opened by its new us subsidiary. yukos chief financial officer bruce misamore who had moved to the us in early december to set up yukos usa acknowledged the point. analysts have questioned whether a us court has any jurisdiction over russian companies while moscow officials have dismissed yukos legal wrangling as meaningless.
