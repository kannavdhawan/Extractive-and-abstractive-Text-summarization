iraq to invite phone licence bids iraq is to invite bids for two telephone licences saying it wants to significantly boost nationwide coverage over the next decade. the ministry said that it wanted to increase iraq s very low telephone service penetration rate from about 4.5 today to about 25 within 10 years. the ministry said it would base its selection on criteria including the speed of implementation tariff rates coverage and the firm s experience and financial strength. bids have been invited from local arab and foreign companies iraq s ministry of communications said. the firms will install and operate a fixed phone network providing voice fax and internet services.
