bmw to recall faulty diesel cars bmw is to recall all cars equipped with a faulty diesel fuel injection pump supplied by parts maker robert bosch. the additional three day stoppage will mean a production loss of up to 3 600 vehicles bmw said adding that it was confident it could make up the numbers later. when asked if bmw would be seeking compensation from bosch the carmaker s chief executive helmut panke said we will first solve the problem before talking about who will pay . the german company is to extend a planned production break at one of its plants due to the faulty bosch part. audi and mercedes benz were also supplied with the defective diesel fuel injection pumps but neither of them have to recall any vehicles.
