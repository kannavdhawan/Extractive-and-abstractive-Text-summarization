borussia dortmund near bust german football club and former european champion borussia dortmund has warned it will go bankrupt if rescue talks with creditors fail. stephen schechter a uk investment banker who has held talks with borussia dortmund over a possible bond sale said the club needs a capital injection of 35m euros. shares in borussia dortmund germany s only stock market listed football club dropped by almost 23 to 2.05 euros during early afternoon trading. it made a loss of 27.2m euros in the first half of the current fiscal year and said that total debts will increase to 134.7m euros by the middle of 2006 unless a restructuring plan is pushed through. fund manager florian hamm borussia dortmund s largest investor said he would only invest more money in the company if he got a greater say in how it is run.
