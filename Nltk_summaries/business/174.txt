yangtze electric's profits double yangtze electric power, the operator of china's three gorges dam, has said its profits more than doubled in 2004. the firm has benefited from increased demand for electricity at a time when power shortages have hit cities and provinces across the country. earlier this month, work was halted on an underground power station, and a supply unit on the three gorges dam, as well as a power station on its sister xiluodu dam because of environmental worries.
