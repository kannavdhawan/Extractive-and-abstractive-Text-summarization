consumers drive french economy france s economic growth accelerated in the last three months of 2004 driven by consumer spending a report shows. france still has a strong economic growth said marc toutai an economist at natexis banques populaires. gross domestic product gdp rose by 0.8 in the fourth quarter compared with the previous three month period the statistical office insee said. consumer spending was up by 1.2 in the fourth quarter and there also was a rebound in business investment that gave the recovery an extra shove. despite the concerns analysts pointed out that france was outperforming the majority of its european counterparts and that its economy was looking more robust than in previous years.
