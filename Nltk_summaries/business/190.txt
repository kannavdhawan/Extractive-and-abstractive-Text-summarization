consumers drive french economy france's economic growth accelerated in the last three months of 2004, driven by consumer spending, a report shows. france still has a strong economic growth, said marc toutai, an economist at natexis banques populaires. it's an economic growth that seems well balanced, said nicolas claquin, an analyst at ccf. another worry is that demand in germany and italy, two of france's main trading partners, is sluggish. gross domestic product gdp rose by 0.8% in the fourth quarter compared with the previous three month period, the statistical office insee said.
