cadbury said its underlying sales were up by 4% in 2004. growth was helped by its confectionery brands including cadbury, trident and halls which enjoyed a successful year, with like for like sales up 6%. cadbury said it was confident it would hit its targets for 2005. while the external commercial environment remains competitive, we are confident that we have the strategy, brands and people to deliver within our goal ranges in 2005, said chief executive todd stitzer.
