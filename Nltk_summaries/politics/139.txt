straw praises kashmir moves the uk has welcomed the decision by india and pakistan to open a bus link across the ceasefire line dividing the disputed region of kashmir. a second bus service linking the pakistani city of lahore with amritsar in india was also announced as well as a rail link between rajasthan state and pakistan s sindh province. on thursday mr straw was in india visiting sikhism s holiest shrine the golden temple in amritsar where he tried his hand at making indian bread or roti. in a statement mr straw said the bus service between srinagar and muzaffarabad will be able to reunite families that have been divided for decades . both sides agreed to begin talks on reducing the risk of nuclear accidents and also said they planned to reopen their respective consulates in karachi and mumbai bombay .
