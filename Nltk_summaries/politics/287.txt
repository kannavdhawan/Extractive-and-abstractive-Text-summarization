boothroyd calls for lords speaker betty boothroyd has said the house of lords needs its own speaker and that peers should lead the way on reforming the upper chamber. baroness boothroyd, who was the first woman to be commons speaker, said she believed tony blair initiated reforms without a clear outcome in mind. i don't go for the idea of somebody a lord chancellor who is head of the judiciary, a senior cabinet minister and speaker of the lords. i think we need to look very carefully at the relationship between the lords and the commons, lord falconer told bbc1's breakfast with frost. i would really like to see a speaker of the house of lords, she said. the lord chancellor currently has the role of house of lords speaker.
