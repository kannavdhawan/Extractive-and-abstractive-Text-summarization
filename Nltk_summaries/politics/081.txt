tory backing for id cards the tories are to back controversial government plans to introduce id cards. sources within the conservative party told the bbc michael howard has always been in favour of id cards and tried to introduce them when he was home secretary. lib dem home affairs spokesman mark oaten said this has all the signs of michael howard overruling colleagues concerns over id cards. despite giving their backing to id cards the conservatives insisted they would hold ministers to account over the precise purpose of the scheme. they said they would also press labour over whether objectives could be met and whether the home office would deliver them.
