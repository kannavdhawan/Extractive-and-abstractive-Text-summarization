ukip outspent labour on eu poll the uk independence party outspent both labour and the liberal democrats in the european elections new figures show. labour s campaign cost 1.7m the lib dems 1.19m and the greens 404 000 according to figures revealed by the electoral commission on wednesday. much of the ukip funding came from yorkshire millionaire sir paul sykes who helped bankroll the party s billboard campaign. ukip which campaigned on a slogan of say no to europe spent 2.36m on the campaign second only to the conservatives 3.13m. but a ukip spokesman said labour and the conservatives had spent 10m between them on the last general election.
