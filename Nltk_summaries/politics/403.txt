voters don t trust politicians eight out of 10 voters do not trust politicians to tell the truth a new poll conducted for the bbc suggests. the lib dems are also keen to highlight the trust issue with charles kennedy has claiming voters had a fundamental lack of trust in the prime minister . but according to the bbc poll 61 said the issue of trust made no difference to whether or not they would vote at the next election widely expected on 5 may. lib dem foreign affairs spokesman menzies campbell said the iraq war had hit trust in politicians hard. former education secretary estelle morris told bbc news 24 that there was a real problem of trust between the public and the politicians.
