blair sets date for africa report the commission for africa s report will be released on 11 march comic relief day tony blair has said. bob geldof in characteristically blunt style promised that the commission would not just be a talking shop but would deliver radical new thinking to change direction for africa. as well as more aid fairer trade and less debt the commission is likely to demand action on corruption in africa. july s g8 summit in gleneagles in perthshire chaired by the prime minister will use the report as the basis for talks on africa. mr blair has vowed to put africa at the top of his agenda during his time at the helm of the g8.
