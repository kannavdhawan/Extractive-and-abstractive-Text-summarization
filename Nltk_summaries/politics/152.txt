minister defends hunting ban law the law banning hunting with dogs in england and wales is enforceable and very clear , alun michael has said. but penny little, who monitored the bicester hunt in oxfordshire, said she had witnessed gratuitous, spiteful killing of foxes . the rural affairs minister said it would become obvious if people flouted the law, which came into force on friday, and pretended they were not. people had turned out to show en masse that the hunting act was a bad law , he said adding that foxes and other animals had been killed legally as far as he was aware. mike hobday, from the league against cruel sports, said video evidence of the law being broken would be passed onto police.
