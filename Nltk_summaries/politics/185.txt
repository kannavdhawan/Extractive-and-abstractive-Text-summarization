police probe bnp mosque leaflet police are investigating a british national party leaflet posted to homes in south wales opposing plans for a new mosque. swansea s muslim community is raising money to open a new mosque and community centre in the former st andrews united reform church. swansea s muslim community bought the former church just across the road from the existing mosque on st helen s road in the late 1990s. andy richards of swansea uaf said the leaflet was exactly what he had come to expect from the bnp. taha idris director of the swansea bay race equality council said his organisation contacted police after receiving complaints from the public.
