mr galloway, who on thursday won 150,000 in libel damages from the daily telegraph said he would contest bethnal green, in london, for respect. speaking from a cafe in brick lane, east london, mr galloway said he had accepted the party's invitation to stand with great honour and pride . the 50 year old mp launched respect, the unity coalition, in january along with 1,000 anti war activists, and the muslim association of britain. mr galloway said that in next year's expected general election and local elections a year later, the party would turn the east end of london into a fortress . in june's european parliament elections, respect failed to gain enough votes for an mep but did come first in tower hamlets borough, most of which is covered by the commons constituency of bethnal green and bow.
