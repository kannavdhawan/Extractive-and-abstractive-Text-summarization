 last chance warning for voters people in england scotland and wales must have registered by 1700 gmt to be able to vote in the general election if it is held as expected on 5 may. as well as for english county councils polls for unitary authorities at bristol isle of wight and stockton on tees and mayors at doncaster hartlepool north tyneside and stoke on trent are also scheduled for 5 may. an electoral commission spokeswoman said political decisions are made on your behalf every day but only by using your right to vote at an election can you really have a say on the issues you care about. there will also be elections for local councils and mayors in parts of england on 5 may. if you want your voice to be heard on 5 may you will need to have registered by friday 11 march.
