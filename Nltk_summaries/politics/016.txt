crisis ahead in social sciences a national body designed to tackle skills shortages in key subjects should be set up a committee of mps has said. the council aims to promote and support strategic research for social science postgraduates and provide social scientists to meet the needs of the country. in its report on the council s work the committee said we are deeply concerned by the skills shortages afflicting in particular the qualitative branches of social sciences. there was an absolute crisis in the recruitment of statisticians and other social science experts the commons science and technology committee added. it is hard to see how significant progress towards rectifying these shortages can be made through the deployment of economic and social research council s limited resources.
