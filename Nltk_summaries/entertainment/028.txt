potter director signs warner deal harry potter director alfonso cuaron has signed a three year deal with hollywood studio warner brothers, according to variety trade magazine. this deal will give us the opportunity to collaborate with alfonso on movies that make the most of his artistry and vision, and continue to offer him the mainstream worldwide audiences that our studio provides so successfully, said mr robinov. the mexican film maker, who directed harry potter and the prisoner of azkaban, will produce mainstream movies and smaller spanish language films. after the erotic nature of y tu mama tambien, cuaron was a surprise choice to direct the third harry potter film but his dark interpretation was received well by the public and critics alike. we had a wonderful experience with alfonso on harry potter, warner producer jeff robinov told variety. it also won him and his brother carlos, with whom he co wrote the screenplay, an oscar nomination for best original screenplay.
