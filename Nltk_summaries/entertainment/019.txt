slater to star in broadway play actor christian slater is stepping into the role of tom in the broadway revival of the glass menagerie. slater 35 is replacing actor dallas roberts in the tennessee williams drama which opens next month. slater won rave reviews for his recent performance in one flew over the cuckoo s nest in london s west end. he has also starred in a number of films including heathers robin hood prince of thieves and more recently churchill the hollywood years. the revival directed by david leveaux will also star jessica lange as the domineering mother amanda wingfield.
