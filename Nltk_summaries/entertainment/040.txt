wine comedy up for six film gongs sideways a wine tasting comedy starring paul giamatti is up for six independent spirit awards the art house version of the oscars. also in the running with two nominations are high school comedy napoleon dynamite the door in the floor and garden state written directed and starring scrubs star zach braff alongside natalie portman. among the other films receiving four nominations apiece were brother to brother a drama about a young gay black man forced to live on the streets robbing peter and primer. controversial biopic kinsey starring liam neeson as sex researcher alfred kinsey was one of four films to get four nominations. these awards for better or worse mean everything said sideways producer michael london adding they were a huge first step toward getting recognition from other awards.
