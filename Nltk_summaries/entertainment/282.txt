rap feud in 50 cent s g unit crew us rap star 50 cent has said he has thrown protege the game out of his g unit gang in a feud that has apparently involved two shootings. 50 cent whose second album is about to be released after his debut made him one of hip hop s biggest stars has been involved in recent rivalries with fellow artists including fat joe nas and jadakiss. when 50 cent appeared on the same station two days later he said the game was no longer a member of g unit. in an interview with hot 97 on saturday the game described some of 50 cent s rivals as my friends and said he would not turn on them. 50 cent s second album the massacre is released on thursday five weeks after the game s debut documentary went to number one.
