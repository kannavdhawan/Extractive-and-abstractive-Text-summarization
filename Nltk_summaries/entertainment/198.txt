brookside actress keaveney dies actress anna keaveney who appeared in brookside my family and a touch of frost has died of lung cancer aged 55. keaveney who played brookside s marie jackson when the channel 4 soap began in 1982 died on saturday. other tv appearances included the part of matron in itv drama footballers wives and a cameo role as tom farrell s mother sheila in bbc comedy gimme gimme gimme. she played nellie in mike leigh s latest film vera drake which won the golden lion prize for best film at this year s venice film festival. keaveney s career also included stage performances in neaptide for the national theatre private lives and the rise and fall of little voice. and most recently she appeared alongside richard wilson in itv s king of fridges and with martin clunes in doc martin.
