nominees for best foreign film include spain's pedro almodovar for bad education and hong kong director wong kar wai for 2046. in the tv categories, comedy series early doors and the alan clark diaries will compete for the best directing award for a 30 minute television show, while the directors of shameless, hustle and bodies are all nominated for the 60 minute television prize. uk directors guild nominees named martin scorsese and clint eastwood are among the nominees for the top prize at the directors guild of great britain awards, now in their second year.
