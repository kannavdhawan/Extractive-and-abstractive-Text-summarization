franz ferdinand's art school lesson scottish rock band franz ferdinand, who shot to prominence in 2004, have won two brit awards. singer alex kapranos was studying english at university but had friends at art school, and the line up was completed when guitarist nick mccarthy moved from munich, germany, to the city. with a cool, spiky, fun sound previously associated with bands like talking heads, they hit upon a style that had been out of fashion for a while and so was ripe for another airing. they signed with independent label domino, home of smog, sebadoh and four tet, in june 2003. their first uk single hit number three last january, followed by two more top 20 hits, while the album has sold more than 600,000 copies in 11 months on release.
