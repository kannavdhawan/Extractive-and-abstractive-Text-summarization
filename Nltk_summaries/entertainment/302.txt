actress roberts takes spider role actress julia roberts will play the part of a spider in a new film version of children s classic charlotte s web. ten year old dakota fanning will play fern the girl at the centre of the story in the film to be directed by 13 going on 30 film maker gary winick. charlotte s web has sold 45 million copies since it was published in 1952. an animated version was made in 1973 but this will be the first live action film. the film a mix of live action and animation will be roberts first project since the birth of her twins hazel and phinnaeus two months ago. oprah winfrey will voice a goose john cleese will voice a sheep and steve buscemi a rat in the 2006 film.
