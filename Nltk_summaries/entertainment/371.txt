arnold congratulated on oscar win oscar winner andrea arnold has been congratulated by the uk film council the organisation which partly funded her project. the uk film council spends millions of pounds of lottery investment on short filmmaking in britain every year said chief executive officer john woodward. arnold s film wasp won the best live action short film award on sunday. wasp which was commissioned by the film council and channel 4 beat films by fellow uk nominees gary mckendry and ashvin kumar to the prize. the film has already won over 30 other international awards including the sundance short film prize.
