tarantino to direct csi episode film director quentin tarantino is to direct an episode of us television series csi crime scene investigation. ms mendelsohn said the production team had been trying for a while to get tarantino to direct an episode of csi, and added that he was a fan of the forensic drama. csi's co producer, carol mendelsohn, said the episode would have more bugs and blood than usual. he knows everything there is to know about csi, and he is into the whole mythology of csi, mendelsohn said. she said he finally agreed a few weeks ago while csi was doing some location shooting in las vegas and the show's stars persuaded him. filming is due to start in early april and the tarantino directed episode will be broadcast in the us on 19 may.
