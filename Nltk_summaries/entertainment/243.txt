blue beat u2 to top france honour irish band u2 have been honoured at france's biggest music awards, but were beaten to a prize by boy band blue. collecting his band's award from model naomi campbell at the cannes ceremony, u2 frontman bono said in french i'm not from this country but i'll make a little confession to you it's at the cote d'azur i feel at home. us band maroon 5 was named best new international artist, and took the best international song title for this love. singer jenifer also took home two awards, for best french female singer and best french album. u2 received a special achievement prize at the nrj music awards, but blue beat them to the international group award.
