jarre joins fairytale celebration french musician jean michel jarre is to perform at a concert in copenhagen to mark the bicentennial of the birth of writer hans christian andersen. the royal couple also visited the hans christian anderson school complex, where queen mary read the ugly duckling to the young audience. there are year long celebrations planned across the world to celebrate andersen and his work, which includes the emperor's new clothes and the little mermaid. christian andersen's fairy tales are timeless and universal, said jarre. later at a gala dinner, danish supermodel helena christensen was named a hans christian andersen ambassador.
