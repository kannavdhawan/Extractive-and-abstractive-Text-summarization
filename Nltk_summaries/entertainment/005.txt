poppins musical gets flying start the stage adaptation of children s film mary poppins has had its opening night in london s west end. mary poppins was originally created by author pamela travers who is said to have cried when she saw disney s 1964 film starring julie andrews. theatre impresario sir cameron mackintosh has said he hopes the musical is a blend of the sweet natured film and the original book. a number of celebrities turned out for the musical s premiere including actress barbara windsor comic graham norton and sir richard attenborough. sir cameron mackintosh s lavish production which has cost 9m to bring to the stage was given a 10 minute standing ovation.
