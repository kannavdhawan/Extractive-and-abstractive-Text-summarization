black friday is based upon the novel of the same name written by journalist s hussain zaidi, which looks at the 15 explosions which rocked mumbai on 12 march 1993. director anurag kashyap said the film's release should not be delayed as the book has already been on sale for two years. their lawyer, majeed memon, said we had argued that how can a film say it is telling the true story when the court itself is struggling to find the truth so we said they should remove the line from their promotional posters and they did give us an assurance that they would do so.
