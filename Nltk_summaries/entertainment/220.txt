new media battle for bafta awards the bbc leads the nominations for the bafta interactive awards, including one for the radio times website and one for sport interactive's euro 2004 coverage. categories include interactive tv, film, digital tv, mobile phones and music. other nominees include the guardian news website, the national theatre, mtv, the science museum and the london stock exchange. shaun of the dead, oasis' definitely maybe, really bend it like beckham, the chaplin collection and the day today will battle it out in the dvd category. the awards, which were started in 1997, recognise the best websites, digital tv shows and cd roms.
