incredibles win animation awards the incredibles movie has beaten shrek 2 to the main prizes at hollywood's animation awards, the annies. tom kenny, who provides the voice for spongebob squarepants, hosted sunday's ceremony at the alex theater, los angeles. the incredibles won a total of 10 awards but shrek 2, which had seven nominations, went home empty handed. spongebob squarepants was named best animated tv show while tv comedy king of the hill picked up two prizes including one for actress brittany murphy's voice work. the superhero film was named best animated feature while brad bird won best director, writer and voice actor for his role as designer edna mode. shrek 2 took 881m 468m around the world, compared with 576m 306m for the incredibles.
