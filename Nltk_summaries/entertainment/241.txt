youssou n dour wins music prize senegalese musician youssou n dour has been named a winner of a bbc radio 3 world music award. it will also include the announcement of the audience award chosen by radio 3 and bbc world service listeners alongside bbc four viewers. the concert will be broadcast on radio 3 s world music day the following evening and will feature performances by many of the award winners. cuban pianist bebo valdes and flamenco singer diego el cigala responsible for critically acclaimed album lagrimas negrasmusic won an award in the boundary crossing category. highlights of the poll winners concert to be held at the sage gateshead will be televised on bbc four on 11 march.
