a listers flock to gervais sitcom hollywood actors samuel l jackson and ben stiller have signed up for ricky gervais new sitcom the comedian has told bbc news. gervais and his writing partner stephen merchant are currently refining the scripts for the six part series in which gervais plays a struggling actor who bitches about the stars. meanwhile gervais is gearing up to promote his cartoon book flanimals which is released in the us in march around the same time as nbc begin showing the us version of the office. he said stiller and jackson would be playing twisted versions of themselves in extras and that the jokes about them would sail pretty close to the bone . gervais admitted he was cautious about revealing who would be taking part until they had all signed on the dotted line.
