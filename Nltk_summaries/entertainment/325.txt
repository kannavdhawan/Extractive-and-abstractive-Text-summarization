bening makes awards breakthrough film actress annette bening is up for an oscar for her starring role in the award winning film being julia. the witty tale of revenge and love set in 1930s london sees the eponymous theatrical diva played by bening grow tired of her success and fall for a young american and could be the role which finally wins her the coveted academy award. bening was the first choice to play the female lead in american beauty director sam mendes film debut which was a critical and commercial success but the actress had to be content with a bafta award for her strong performance. following a cameo in postcards from the edge bening s breakthrough role came in 1990 playing seductive con artist myra langtry in the grifters a role that won her an oscar nomination. bening played virginia hill another role which won her high critical acclaim but the film only picked up two of a staggering 12 oscar nominations.
