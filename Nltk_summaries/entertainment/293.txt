singer ian brown in gig arrest former stone roses singer ian brown was arrested after a fight during a concert in san francisco on tuesday his spokesman has said. brown 42 was lead singer with the stone roses one of the most seminal bands in british rock until they split in 1996. he has since forged a successful solo career scoring nine uk top 30 singles since 1998. in 1998 he was sentenced to four months in jail for using threatening behaviour towards an aeroplane captain and stewardess. a fan jumped on stage and attacked the singer who then became involved in a fracas with a security guard fiction records spokesman paul smernicki said. police took witness statements and apprehended brown at his hotel mr smernicki said. brown then got into a brawl with another man who tried to restrain him without realising he was a security guard mr smernicki added.
