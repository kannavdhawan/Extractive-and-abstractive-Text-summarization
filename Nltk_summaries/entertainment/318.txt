roxy music on isle of wight bill roxy music will appear at june's isle of wight music festival, along with morrissey, supergrass and idlewild. roxy music's back catalogue was re released in 2000, leading to the band undertaking a world tour in 2001. morrissey will also play on saturday at the festival, while supergrass and idlewild have been confirmed for the friday. their first hit was 1972's virginia plain, with other hits including street life, love is the drug and the 1981 john lennon cover, jealous guy. original band members bryan ferry, phil manzerana, andy mackay and paul thompson will take to the stage on the saturday night of the festival. roxy music were formed in 1971 by ferry, mackay and brian eno, with manzanera joining the following year.
