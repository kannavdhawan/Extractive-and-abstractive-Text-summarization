prodigy join v festival line up essex act prodigy are to headline the second stage at this year's v festival, joining main stage headliners scissor sisters and franz ferdinand. a month later at the v festival, prodigy will play at weston park on saturday 20 august and hylands park on sunday 21 august and the chemical brothers vice versa. the event, which is in its 10th year, will be held at two venues hylands park in essex and weston park in staffordshire on 20 and 21 august. v festival director bob angus said with the prodigy and the chemical brothers now confirmed to headline the second stage, we are headed for a really stellar line up. other bands at the sold out festival include queens of the stone age, the killers, keane, the streets and foo fighters.
