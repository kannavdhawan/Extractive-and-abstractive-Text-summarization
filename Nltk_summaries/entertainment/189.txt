holmes wins '2004 top tv moment' sprinter kelly holmes' olympic victory has been named the top television moment of 2004 in a bbc poll. double gold medal winner holmes topped the best sports moment category, beating maria sharapova's wimbledon triumph and matthew pinsent's rowing victory at the olympics. holmes' 800m gold medal victory beat favourite moments from drama, comedy and factual programmes, as voted by television viewers. michael buerk's return to ethiopia, 20 years after originally reporting its famine, topped the factual category for bbc programme this world. the sight of former royal correspondent jennie bond with dozens of rats crawling over her in itv's i'm a celebrity get me out of here was named best factual entertainment moment.
