films on war triumph at sundance a study of the united states at war in the past 50 years has picked up one of the main awards at the 2005 sundance film festival in utah in the us. in the new world cinema drama category the angolan film the hero triumphed to win the grand jury prize. meanwhile french israeli production wall which looks at israel s controversial security wall separating it from the palestinian territories picked up a world cinema special jury prize for documentaries. the sundance festival was founded by actor robert redford in 1981. this year s festival which ended on sunday after a 11 day run has been dominated by the themes of war and politics. why we fight scooped the grand jury prize for documentaries at the world s leading independent film festival.
