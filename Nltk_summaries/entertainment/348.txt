berlin celebrates european cinema organisers say this year s berlin film festival which opens on thursday with period epic man to man will celebrate a revitalised european cinema. some 350 films will be screened at this year s festival with a further 300 shown at the european film market that runs alongside it. festival director dieter kosslick says this strong showing signals a new consciousness for european films . i m not worried that we won t be able to fill the red carpet with stars he said though he admitted the festival may be moved to january next year to avoid a similar clash. of the 21 films in competition for the golden and silver bear awards more than half are from europe with france particularly well represented.
