byrds producer melcher dies at 62 record producer terry melcher, who was behind hits by the byrds, ry cooder and the beach boys, has died aged 62. the son of actress doris day, he helped write kokomo for the beach boys, which was used in the movie cocktail, earning a 1988 golden globe nomination. earlier in his career, melcher had hits as part of duo called bruce terry, with future beach boy bruce johnston, which evolved into the rip chords group.
