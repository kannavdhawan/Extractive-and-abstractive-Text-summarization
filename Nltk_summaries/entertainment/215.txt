last year, the south bank award for best tv drama went to steven poliakoff's period piece the lost prince, while bloody sunday, about the 1972 killings in northern ireland, won in 2003. in other south bank categories, little britain's second series beat nighty night and the green wing to the comedy trophy while alan bennett's the history boys won in the theatre category. shameless, state of play and clocking off creator paul abbott got a lifetime achievement award while former grange hill actress amma asante won the breakthrough award for writing and directing her first film, a way of life.
