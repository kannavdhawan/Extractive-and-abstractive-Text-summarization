snow patrol feted at irish awards snow patrol were the big winners in ireland's top music honours, the meteor awards, picking up accolades for best irish band and album on thursday. in the international categories, morrissey beat eminem, usher and robbie williams to best male while pj harvey pipped kylie minogue, joss stone, anastacia and natasha bedingfield to the female crown. westlife won the award for best irish pop act, voted for by the public, beating former member brian mcfadden. snow patrol will support u2 on their european tour later this year but u2 were not nominated for best irish band and album. franz ferdinand picked up best international band and album while paddy casey collected best irish male.
