brit awards for devon music acts devon singer joss stone and rock band muse won coveted brit awards during the event s 25th anniversary. seventeen year old stone from near cullumpton won two awards best british female act and best urban act out of three award nominations. her second album mind body soul reached number one in the uk charts last october and went straight into the us charts at number 11. the teenager also has grammy nominations in the us normally dominated by home grown acts. muse whose members met in south devon beat franz ferdinand jamie cullum kasabian and the libertines for best british live act. after beating amy winehouse jamelia natasha bedingfield and pj harvey to the best british female prize joss stone said i don t know what to say.
