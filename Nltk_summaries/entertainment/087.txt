global release for japan hit film oscar winning animator hayao miyazaki's latest film, howl's moving castle, is to be released in 50 countries around the world, its distributor has said. howl's moving castle set a japanese box office record last week, taking 1.5bn yen 7.7m in its first two days. howl's moving castle is based on a children's fantasy novel by uk author diana wynne jones. she heads to a moving castle kept by howl, a wizard, and searches for a way to become normal again. miyazaki won an oscar earlier this year for spirited away, japan's first academy award for nearly 50 years. the film's worldwide release will start in south korea on 24 december, and france on 12 january.
