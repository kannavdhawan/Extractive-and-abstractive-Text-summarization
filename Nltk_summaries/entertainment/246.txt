singer sizzla jailed for swearing reggae star sizzla, whose uk tour was cancelled after protests at his anti gay lyrics, has been jailed in jamaica for swearing on stage. one of his most controversial songs, pump up, contains the jamaican patois lyrics fire fi di man dem weh go ride man behind , translated by gay rights group outrage as burn the men who have sex with men . sizzla, whose real name is miguel collins, has released 25 albums since 1995 and is credited with taking dancehall music back to its reggae origins. the singer must serve 15 days in prison after he consistently swore during a concert in st thomas, jamaica, in january despite warnings by police. sizzla's uk tour was cancelled after scotland yard's racial and violent crime taskforce announced it was examining lyrics by eight reggae artists.
