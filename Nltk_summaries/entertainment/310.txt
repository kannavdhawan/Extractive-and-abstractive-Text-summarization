bbc denies blackadder tv comeback the bbc has said there are no plans in the pipeline for a new series of hit comedy blackadder, which ended in 1989. tony robinson, who played the servant baldrick, told itv1's this morning the show's star, rowan atkinson, was more keen than he has been in the past . robinson added he would love to do another series, each of which was set in a different era, ranging from the 15th century to world war i. but the bbc said on thursday there were no plans for a comeback.
