born in luton, bedfordshire, on 5 april 1920, hailey was the only child of working class parents, they could not afford to keep him in school beyond the age of 14. he served as a pilot with the royal air force during world war ii, flying fighter planes to the middle east. strong medicine, hailey's blockbuster focusing on the pharmaceutical industry, was turned into a film in 1986, starring sam neill, douglas fairbanks jr and a panoply of former soap stars.
