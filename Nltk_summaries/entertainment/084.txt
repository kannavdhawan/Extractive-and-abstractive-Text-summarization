oscar nominees gear up for lunch leonardo dicaprio, jamie foxx and hilary swank are among those due to attend this year's oscar nominees luncheon on monday. oscar nominees swank and foxx were among the winners at the screen actors guild awards at the weekend, one of the many ceremonies held in the run up to the oscars. martin scorsese's the aviator is leading the field at this year's oscars with 11 nominations, while other multiple nominees include million dollar baby and sideways. they will join more than 100 nominees from the 24 oscar categories at the annual event, which will take place at the beverly hilton hotel, los angeles. many of those nominated for oscars including dicaprio, foxx and staunton an oscar nominee for her performance in vera drake have also been nominated for baftas.
