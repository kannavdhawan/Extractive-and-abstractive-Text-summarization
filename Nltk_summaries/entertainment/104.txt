glasgow hosts tsunami benefit gig the top names in scottish music are taking part in a benefit concert in aid of the victims of the asian tsunami. colin macintyre of mull historical society was playing another gig on the same day but said he was determined to make the glasgow benefit. and it is taking place on the same night as a tsunami benefit show in bristol which will see massive attack and portishead share a stage for the first time. all 10 000 tickets for saturday s concert featuring franz ferdinand belle and sebastian and travis at glasgow s secc sold out in 36 hours. mull historical society deacon blue idlewild texas mogwai and teenage fanclub are among the other acts performing at the concert.
