past eurovision performers include abba, celine dion, bucks fizz, nana mouskouri, lulu and julio iglesias. the first contest was organised by the european broadcasting union, or ebu, in 1956, and has become an annual event pitting pop giants against musical minnows with often surprising results. the 50th annual contest will be held in kiev, ukraine, in may, after ukrainian singer ruslana won last year. ireland has been the most successful country in the show's history with seven victories, followed by the uk, france and luxembourg with five each. eurovision 'greats' to do battle stars of the eurovision song contest from the show's 50 year history are to compete against each other to celebrate the contest's anniversary.
