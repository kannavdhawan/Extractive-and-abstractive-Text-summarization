broadband is moving the perception of the internet as a piece of technology to an integral part of home life in the uk, said karen thomson, chief executive of aol uk, with many people spending time on their computers as automatically as they might switch on the television or radio. according to analysts nielsen netratings, more than 50% of the 22.8 million uk net users regularly accessing the web from home each month are logging on at high speed they spend twice as long online than people on dial up connections, viewing an average of 1,444 pages per month.
