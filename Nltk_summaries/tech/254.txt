broadband in the uk gathers pace one person in the uk is joining the internet's fast lane every 10 seconds, according to bt. the take up rate for broadband is accelerating at a terrific pace, said ben verwaayen, bt's chief executive. according to bt, more than 95% of uk homes and businesses can receive broadband over the phone line. part of the surge in people signing up was due to bt stretching the reach of adsl the uk's most widely used way of getting broadband beyond six kilometres. including those connected via cable, almost six million people have a fast, always on connection.
