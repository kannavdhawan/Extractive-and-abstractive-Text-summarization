broadband in the uk gathers pace one person in the uk is joining the internet s fast lane every 10 seconds according to bt. part of the surge in people signing up was due to bt stretching the reach of adsl the uk s most widely used way of getting broadband beyond six kilometres. those signing up to broadband include those that get their service direct from bt or via the many companies that re sell bt lines under their own name. the telecoms giant said the number of people on broadband via the telephone line had now surpassed four million. according to bt more than 95 of uk homes and businesses can receive broadband over the phone line.
