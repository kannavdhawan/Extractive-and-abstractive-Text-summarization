british library gets wireless net visitors to the british library will be able to get wireless internet access alongside the extensive information available in its famous reading rooms. at the british library we are continually exploring ways in which technology can help us to improve services to our users said lynne brindley chief executive of the british library. the british library receives around 3 000 visitors each day and serves around 500 000 readers each year. surveys we conducted recently confirmed that alongside the materials they consult here our users want to be able to access the internet when they are at the library for research or to communicate with colleagues she said. the majority of visitors wanted to be able to access their e mail as well as the british library catalogue.
