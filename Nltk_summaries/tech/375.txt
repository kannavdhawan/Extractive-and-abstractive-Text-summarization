more than twice as many laptops were left in the back of black cabs in london as in any of the nine other cities helsinki, oslo, munich, paris, stockholm, copenhagen, chicago and sydney where the research into lost and found gadgets was carried out. this has left londoners, or those travelling by cab in the capital, as the world's best at losing laptops, according to the research by the licensed taxi drivers association and pointsec, a mobile data backup firm.
