writing in the journal nature, dr paniccia and colleagues haisheng rong, richard jones, ansheng liu, oded cohen, dani hak and alexander fang show how they have made a continuous laser from the same material used to make computer processors. currently, says dr paniccia, telecommunications equipment that amplifies the laser light that travels down fibre optic cables is very expensive because of the exotic materials, such as gallium arsenide, used to make it.
