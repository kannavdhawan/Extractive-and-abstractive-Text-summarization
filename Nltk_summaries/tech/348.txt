game makers get xbox 2 sneak peek microsoft has given game makers a glimpse of the new xbox 2 console. mr allard said microsoft had to work hard to ensure that it was easy for game makers to produce titles for the xbox 2 and for players to get playing. the xbox would also support well known industry specifications such as directx to make it simple for game studios to make titles for the console. also all titles for the new xbox will have the same interface to make it easy to play online and buy extras for characters or other add ons for games. this interface will hold details of a player s statistics and skill level on a gamer card as well as give access to a store where people can spend small amounts of cash to buy extras for their avatars or add ons such as new maps or vehicles for games they possess.
