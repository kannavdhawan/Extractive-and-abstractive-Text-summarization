us woman sues over cartridges a us woman is suing hewlett packard hp saying its printer ink cartridges are secretly programmed to expire on a certain date. the lawsuit seeks to represent anyone in the us who has purchased an hp inkjet printer since february 2001. hp the world s biggest printer firm declined to comment on the lawsuit. the smart chip is dually engineered to prematurely register ink depletion and to render a cartridge unusable through the use of a built in expiration date that is not revealed to the consumer the suit said. the price of inkjet printers has come down to as little as 34 but it could cost up to 1 700 in running costs over an 18 month period due to cartridge a study by computeractive magazine revealed last year. hp ink cartridges use a chip technology to sense when they are low on ink and advise the user to make a change.
