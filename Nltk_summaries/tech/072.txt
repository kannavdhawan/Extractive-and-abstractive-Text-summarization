we are increasingly finding companies that not only want to provide neat, hi tech devices, but also bundle with it a hi tech, efficient power supply, the epa's andrew fanara said. they provide vital juice for billions of mobile phones, pdas personal digital assistants , digital cameras, camcorders, and digital music players. the biggest culprit, according to the epa, is the innocuous power adaptor, nicknamed energy vampires . initiatives like this are critical if power adaptors continue to be made and used as they are now, consumer electronics and other small appliances will be responsible for more than 40% of electricity used in us homes, said the epa. ebay's initiative pulls together major technology firms, environment groups, government agencies and ebay users to give information about what to do with old computers and where to send them. there is more and more awareness on the consumer side, but the whole industry is moving towards being lead free, intel's allen wilson told the bbc news website.
