digital uk driven by net and tv the uk s adoption of digital tv and broadband has helped make it the fourth most digitally savvy nation in europe according a report by jupiter research. the european digital life index demonstrates that digital lifestyles are common today but across europe there is no single digital lifestyle said nate elliott jupiter analyst. jupiter research s index is calculated using 40 different variables across net users digital tv adoption wireless and mobile online activity and digital devices. cable company ntl is trialling faster adsl asymmetric digital subscriber line broadband technology using adsl2 which can give speeds of 18mbps compared to current speeds which are usually around 1mbps or 2mbps. the least digital of the european nations was greece in 17th position according to the digital life index.
