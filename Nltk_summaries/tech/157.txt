when i started researching political games at the university, about five years ago, i thought it was going to be something that would take decades to happen, said gonzalo frasca, computer games specialist at the information technology university of copenhagen. more than 20 followed suit, including frontrunner, elections, president forever and the political machine, which allowed players to run an entire presidential campaign, including having to cope with the media. online games play with politics after bubbling under for some time, online games broke through onto the political arena in 2004. the us presidential election provided a showcase for many, aimed at talking directly to a generation that has grown up with joysticks and gamepads. i must admit that i was the first person to be surprised at seeing how fast they have evolved, added the uruguayan born researcher, who has so far created games for two political campaigns. video games generate strong reactions mainly because they are new, but also because our culture needs to learn how to deal with simulation, mr frasca told the bbc news website.
