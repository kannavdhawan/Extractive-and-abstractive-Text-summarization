half life 2 sweeps bafta awards pc first person shooter half life 2 has won six bafta awards, including best game and best online game. the game also won baftas for best action adventure, best pc game, art direction and animation. grant dean, chairman of the bafta games awards, said at a ceremony in london on tuesday the last year has been a great year for the interactive entertainment industry. halo 2 won the best xbox game category, while prince of persia warrior within was adjudged the best gamecube title. burnout 3 won three awards in the categories for racing, technical direction and best playstation 2 game. the sports award went to konami's pro evolution soccer 4. bafta said the significant feature of this year's awards was the number of non traditional games .
