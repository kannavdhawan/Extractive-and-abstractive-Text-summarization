balco case trial date pushed back the trial date for the bay area laboratory cooperative balco steroid distribution case has been postponed.balco founder victor conte along with james valente, coach remy korchemny and trainer greg anderson are charged with distributing steroids to athletes.the balco defence team have already lost their appeal to have the case dismissed at a pre trial hearing in san francisco but will still argue the case should not go to trial.the hearing in june will focus on the admissibility of evidence gathered during police raids on balco's offices and anderson's home.balco has been accused by the united states anti doping agency usada of being the source of the banned steroid thg and modafinil.
