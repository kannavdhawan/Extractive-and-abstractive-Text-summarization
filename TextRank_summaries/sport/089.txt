thanou desperate to make return greek sprinter katerina thanou says she is eager to compete again after being cleared of missing a drugs test by an independent greek tribunal.thanou, 30, was provisionally suspended for missing a test before the olympics, but the decision was overturned.the iaaf will decide if we can compete again in greece and abroad, thanou told to vima newspaper in her first interview since the athens olympics.thanou, 30, and her compatriot kostas kenteris were provisionally suspended by the iaaf in december for missing three drugs tests.but an independent tribunal of the greek athletics federation overturned the provisional ban on 18 march.the iaaf which said it was very surprised by the decision of the greek tribunal is deciding whether to appeal against the decision at the court of arbitration for sport.
