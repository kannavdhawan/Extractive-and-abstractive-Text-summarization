mcilroy continues winning streak james mcilroy stormed to his second international victory in less than a week, claiming the men's 800m at the teag indoor meeting in erfurt.the northern ireland runner set a new personal best of one minute, 46.68 seconds a time good enough to qualify for the european indoor championships.mcilroy is now hoping to gain a late entry into sunday's international indoor meeting in leipzig.and his time in erfurt makes him third fastest over 800m in the world this year.
