serena ends sania mirza's dream sania mirza, the first indian woman to reach the third round of a grand slam tennis event, has lost to women's favourite serena williams.the 18 year old mirza, who got a wild card entry into the australian open in melbourne, lost to williams 1 6,4 6 in the third round.the only other indian woman to win a match at a grand slam is nirupama vaidyanathan.mirza, a junior wimbledon doubles title winner, became the first indian woman to reach the third round of a grand slam tennis event when she beat hungarian petra mandula on wednesday.i was confident but i didn't think it was going to be that easy, mirza said after her second round win.tennis is not a particularly popular sport in india, but a number of indians watched the live telecast of the match between mirza and williams.
