captains lining up for aid match ireland's brian o'driscoll is one of four six nations captains included in the northern hemisphere squad for the irb rugby aid match on 5 march.france's fabien pelous, gordon bullock of scotland and italy's marco bortolami are also in the northern party.sir clive woodward will coach the northern team against rod macqueen's southern hemisphere team in a tsumani fund raising match at twickenham.it will be a chance for woodward to assess some of his options before unveiling his british and irish lions touring party, who will visit new zealand in the summer.
