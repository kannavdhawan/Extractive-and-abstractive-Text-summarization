radcliffe eyes hard line on drugs paula radcliffe has called for all athletes found guilty on drugs charges to be treated as criminals.the marathon world record holder believes more needs to be done to rid athletics of the suspicions and innuendoes which greet any fast time.doping in sport is a criminal offence and should be treated as such, the 30 year old told the sunday times.often, when an athlete puts in a good performance, they are subjected to suspicions and innuendoes instead of praise.
