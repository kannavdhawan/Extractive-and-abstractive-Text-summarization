england given tough sevens draw england will have to negotiate their way through a tough draw if they are to win the rugby world cup sevens in hong kong next month.the second seeds have been drawn against samoa, france, italy, georgia and chinese taipei.the top two sides in each pool qualify but england could face 2001 winners new zealand in the quarter finals if they stumble against samoa.england have a fantastic record in hong kong and have won there the last three years, but the world cup is on a different level.england won the first rugby world cup sevens in 1993 with a side that included the likes of lawrence dallaglio and matt dawson.england 2 , samoa 7 , france, italy, georgia, chinese taipei.
