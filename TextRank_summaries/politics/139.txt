straw praises kashmir moves the uk has welcomed the decision by india and pakistan to open a bus link across the ceasefire line dividing the disputed region of kashmir.foreign secretary jack straw, touring south east asian countries, praised the spirit of cooperation in achieving the breakthrough.kashmiri politicians on both sides of the line of control which divides the region welcomed the move.in a statement, mr straw said the bus service between srinagar and muzaffarabad will be able to reunite families that have been divided for decades .this will make a real difference to the lives of kashmiris on both sides of the line of control, he said.a second bus service linking the pakistani city of lahore with amritsar in india was also announced as well as a rail link between rajasthan state and pakistan's sindh province.
