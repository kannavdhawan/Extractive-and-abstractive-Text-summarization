a government commissioned study ranked the uk third in its world index of use of information and communication technology ict .mr o'brien said uk businesses were sprinting forward in ict use, but that there were more challenges ahead.it showed british business brought greater maturity to their ict use, by using broadband in increased numbers, bringing ict into their business plans and using new technologies such as voice activated programmes and desktop video conferences.mr o'brien said the increase in the proportion of business connected by broadband shows that uk companies are embracing the opportunities that ict can bring.the government would play its part in cultivating an environment where information and communication technologies can flourish , mr o'brien said.
