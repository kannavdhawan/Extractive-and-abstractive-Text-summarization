kennedy questions trust of blair lib dem leader charles kennedy has said voters now have a fundamental lack of trust of tony blair as prime minister.he said backing his party was not a wasted vote, adding that with the lib dems what you see is what you get .mr kennedy said during his nearly 22 years in parliament he had seen prime ministers and party leaders come and go and knew the pitfalls of british politics.we don't actually hear the argument any longer 'lib dems, good people, reasonable ideas but only if we thought they could win around here it's a wasted vote'.there is a fundamental lack of trust in tony blair as prime minister and in his government, he said.
