artists' secret postcards on sale postcards by artists including damien hirst and tracey emin have sold just hours after the opening of the royal academy of arts annual secrets sale.there are still some big names left, such as mario testino, said rca spokeswoman sue bradburn.the sale opened at 8am on friday and will close at 6pm on saturday.ms bradburn said there was a big queue at the start of the sale but it had now gone down.she said the people that had bought the famous name postcards had arrived early and had spent time studying each work.
