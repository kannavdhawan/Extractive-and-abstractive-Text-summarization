hendrix guitar fetches 100,000 a much loved guitar belonging to jimi hendrix has been sold for 100,000 at an auction in london.the 1965 fender stratocaster was one of a number of guitars included in the sale dedicated to the rock legend.the vast archive of instruments, signed records and posters were collected by a dedicated fan who had become friendly with the star.the jimi hendrix experience's first single hey joe, signed by all the band, was sold for 2,000.
