viotti, director of la fenice since 2002, conducted at renowned opera houses worldwide including milan's la scala and the vienna state opera.at la fenice viotti was widely acclaimed for his production of the french composer massenet's thais and some of his other productions included giuseppe verdi's la traviata and richard strauss's ariadne auf naxos.the last opera he directed at la fenice was massenet's le roi de lahore.viotti's debut at the new york's metropolitan opera came in 2000 with giacomo puccini's madame butterfly, followed by la boheme, la traviata and fromental halevy's la juive.viotti's last public performance was on 5 february when he conducted vincenzo bellini's norma at the vienna state opera.
