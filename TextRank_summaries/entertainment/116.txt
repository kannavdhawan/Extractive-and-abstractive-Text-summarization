the hit factory, which opened 37 years ago, has played host to some of the biggest stars in music, including paul simon, madonna and david bowie.lennon recorded his final album double fantasy at the studio in 1979.other well known albums to be recorded or partially recorded at the studio include bruce springsteen's born in the usa, the rolling stones' emotional rescue and paul simon's graceland.the studio made history in 1994 when it won a record 41 grammy nominations for songs recorded, mastered or mixed there, including the soundtrack to the whitney houston film the bodyguard.
