youssou n'dour wins music prize senegalese musician youssou n'dour has been named a winner of a bbc radio 3 world music award.a special poll winners concert, hosted by eliza carthy and benjamin zephaniah will take place at the sage in gateshead on 5 march.the concert will be broadcast on radio 3's world music day the following evening and will feature performances by many of the award winners.highlights of the poll winners concert, to be held at the sage, gateshead, will be televised on bbc four on 11 march.
