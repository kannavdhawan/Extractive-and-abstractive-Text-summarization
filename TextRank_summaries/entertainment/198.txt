brookside actress keaveney dies actress anna keaveney, who appeared in brookside, my family and a touch of frost, has died of lung cancer aged 55.keaveney, who played brookside's marie jackson when the channel 4 soap began in 1982, died on saturday.other tv appearances included the part of matron in itv drama footballers' wives and a cameo role as tom farrell's mother sheila in bbc comedy gimme gimme gimme.
