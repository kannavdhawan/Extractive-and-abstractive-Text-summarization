french honour for director parker british film director sir alan parker has been made an officer in the order of arts and letters, one of france's highest cultural honours.sir alan received his decoration in paris on wednesday from french culture minister renaud donnedieu de vabres.he told the minister i am honoured to be thus distinguished by france, the flag carrier of cinema throughout the world.through your work and your campaigns, you have shown us how the artist occupies an essential place in our contemporary society, mr de vabres said.
