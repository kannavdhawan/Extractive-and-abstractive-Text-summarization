john peel replacement show begins the permanent replacement for late dj john peel's bbc radio 1 show goes on air on tuesday, with three hosts chosen to fill the legendary presenter's slot.rock dj huw stephens will go on air in the 2300 slot every tuesday, with black music champion ras kwame on wednesdays and dance dj rob da bank on thursdays.radio 1 said the station was not trying to replace peel, but would rise to the challenge of keeping his legacy alive with unpredictable music.the three djs were chosen for their in depth musical knowledge across a variety of musical genres , the station said.rob da bank is one of the hosts of the blue room, an early morning weekend show which plays electronic and dance music.and ras kwame is host of 100% homegrown on radio 1's digital station 1xtra, dedicated to showcasing the best hip hop and garage.this time next year i hope we are saying how well these djs have done, but i fear this time next year we will really realise how much john peel will be forever missed.very good idea to continue the legacy without attempting to replace john peel.bbc radio should seek out the real talent from internet radio stations, people who are making a real impact on the world of music and the world in general.
