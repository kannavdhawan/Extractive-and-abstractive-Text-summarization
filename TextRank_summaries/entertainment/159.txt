band aid 20 single storms to no 1 the new version of the band aid song do they know it's christmas has gone straight in at number one in the uk singles chart.the original version which was the christmas number one in 1984 sold 750,000 copies in its first week and 3.5 million in total.however, band aid 20 is not going to be released in the us, despite being sold in many countries around the world.british stars who appear on the current recording, such as dido and coldplay's chris martin, are well known to music fans across the atlantic, along with u2 frontman bono.in 1985, a group of high profile american stars known as usa for africa came together to record their own fund raising single, we are the world.the band aid trust which was set up 20 years ago, when the original single was released, handed out 144m 75m to famine relief projects across africa between january 1985 and november 2004.
