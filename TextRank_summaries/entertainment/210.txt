muslim group attacks tv drama 24 a british muslim group has criticised the new series of us drama 24, which is about to be aired on sky one, claiming it portrays islam unfairly.the muslim council of britain has complained to broadcasting watchdog ofcom.the muslim council of britain said in a statement we are greatly concerned by the unremittingly hostile and unbalanced portrayal of muslims in this series of 24 based upon a preview of the first five episodes that we have seen.but sky denied the programme breached broadcasting guidelines.sky does not believe that the episodes that it has reviewed to date breach ofcom's programme code.
