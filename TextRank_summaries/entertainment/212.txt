little britain two top comic list little britain stars matt lucas and david walliams have been named the most powerful people in tv comedy, in a poll by listings magazine radio times.newcomers to the annual list included nighty night star julia davis at seven and black books' tamsin greig at 19.lucas and walliams won numerous awards for little britain in 2004, including national television, royal television society and british comedy awards.they topped the radio times list after rising from 10th position last year.
