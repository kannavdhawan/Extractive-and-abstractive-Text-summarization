last star wars 'not for children' the sixth and final star wars movie may not be suitable for young children, film maker george lucas has said.he told us tv show 60 minutes that revenge of the sith would be the darkest and most violent of the series.lucas predicted the film would get a us rating advising parents some scenes may be unsuitable for under 13s.the five previous star wars films have all carried less restrictive pg parental guidance ratings in the us.
