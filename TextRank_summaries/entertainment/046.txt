howl helps boost japan's cinemas japan's box office received a 3.8% boost last year, with ticket sales worth 211bn yen 1.08bn .the surge was led by animated movie howl's moving castle, which took 20bn yen 102m to become the biggest film in japan in 2004.the last samurai, starring tom cruise, was the biggest foreign movie hit in japan last year, taking 13.8bn yen 70.7m .japanese films accounted for 37.5% of japan's box office total last year, with foreign films taking the remaining 62.5%.
