tarantino 'to make friday sequel' director quentin tarantino is in talks to write and direct a new instalment in the friday the 13th horror franchise, according to the hollywood reporter.the original film, released in 1980, has spawned ten sequels based around mask wearing murderer jason voorhees.that film saw jason battle freddy krueger, star of the nightmare on elm street franchise.tarantino is said to be intrigued by the prospect of building a new film around one of the horror genre's most recognised figures.
