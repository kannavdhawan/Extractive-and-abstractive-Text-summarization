veteran comic cyril fletcher dies veteran comedian and broadcaster cyril fletcher has died aged 91 at his home in guernsey.that's life host esther rantzen said he was so lovely and a delight to work with.one of the last comedians of old time variety era, fletcher made his first tv appearance in 1937.he went on to become a regular voice on radio, have a string of tv shows in the 1950s and appear in several films, including 1947's nicholas nickleby.in the mid 1990s, fletcher said he had made a fabulous living since the age of 22 and had never had a day out of work.fletcher was described as a consummate professional by his friend and fellow broadcaster michael pointon.it really was a pleasure to work with him he had met everyone and done everything, mr pointon said.fletcher's daughter jill said her father would be sorely missed.
