sky takes over oscar night mantle sky has signed a major new deal to broadcast this year's academy awards, taking over from three years of live oscar coverage on the bbc.sky said it was honoured to have won exclusive broadcasting rights and promised to cover the event in a way no other broadcaster could .there will also be a month long season of oscar winning films on sky movies in the run up to the event and live uninterrupted coverage on the night.we are honoured to have won the live and exclusive broadcasting rights for the academy awards, said sky networks' sophie turner laing.
