wine comedy up for six film gongs sideways, a wine tasting comedy starring paul giamatti, is up for six independent spirit awards, the art house version of the oscars.the awards, now in their 20th year, honour quirky low budget films, all of which must have a degree of independent financing.sideways is written and directed by alexander payne, who directed the 2002 hit about schmidt, winning jack nicholson his 12th academy award nomination.among the other films receiving four nominations apiece were brother to brother, a drama about a young gay black man forced to live on the streets, robbing peter and primer.
