bollywood dvd fraudster is jailed a major distributor of pirated dvds of bollywood films has been sent to prison for three years.buhecha, who made 26,000 per month from his illegal trade, was called one of the biggest bollywood pirates in the uk by the sentencing judge.in december 2002, he was stopped in his car by trading standards officers, who uncovered 1,000 pirated dvds and faked inlay cards printed with registered trademarks.his home and a lock up in cambridge were found to contain 18,000 counterfeit dvds and further faked inlay cards.legitimate bollywood film distributors have hailed the conviction as a major boost .the bpi welcomed the news of the prison sentence, but warned there are plenty of other active counterfeiters of bollywood films.
