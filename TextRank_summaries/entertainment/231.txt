three djs replace peel radio show the late john peel's bbc radio 1 show is to be succeeded in february by three shows hosted by three djs focusing on diverse, non commercial music.huw stephens, ras kwame and rob da bank will each host the mid week, late night timeslot, showcasing uk talent.radio 1 said the show would not try to replace peel, but would rise to the challenge of keeping his legacy alive with unpredictable music.rob da bank has been hosting the john peel show since the dj's death.huw stephens is currently one half of the radio 1 thursday night show bethan and huw in wales, which explores new music, especially up and coming acts breaking through in wales.it is widely accepted that john peel can never be replaced, said the radio station.stephens said he grew up listening to john and that it was a massive honour to continue peel's work championing new music.
