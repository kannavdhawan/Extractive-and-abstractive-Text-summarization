kidman wins photographer battle actress nicole kidman has won a restraining order against two paparazzi photographers who she claims left her fearful of leaving her sydney mansion.kidman was prompted to take action following a reported high speed car chase with members of the paparazzi in sydney last weekend.according to local newspaper the daily telegraph, the incident involved paparazzi driving through red lights and on the wrong side of the road in pursuit of kidman's vehicle.lawyers for mr fawcett and mr mcdonald denied that the pair were involved in the chase.magistrate lee gilmore, who issued the restraining order at waverley local court in sydney, said she understood the photographers were entitled to earn a living but there had to be limits to their behaviour.
