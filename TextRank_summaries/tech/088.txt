voters flock to blog awards site voting is under way for the annual bloggies which recognise the best web blogs online spaces where people publish their thoughts of the year.weblogs have been nominated in 30 categories, from the top regional blog, to the best kept secret blog.nikolai nolan, who has run the bloggies for the past five years, told the bbc news website he was not too surprised by the amount of voters who crowded the site.there were many new finalists this year, he added, and a few that had won bloggies before.there are four nominations for the south east asia earthquake and tsunami blog, which is a pretty timely one for 2005, said mr nolan.the nominated blogs are wide ranging covering what is in the news to quirky sites of interest.in a sign that blogs are playing an increasingly key part in spreading news and current affairs, the south east asia earthquake and tsunami blog is also nominated in the best overall category.nominations include flickr, a web photo album which lets people upload, tag, share and publish their images to blogs.three new categories have been added to the list this year, including best food, best entertainment, and best writing of a weblog.one of the categories that was scrapped though was best music blog.
