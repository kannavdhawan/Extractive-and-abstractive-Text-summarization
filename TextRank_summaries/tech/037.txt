us woman sues over cartridges a us woman is suing hewlett packard hp , saying its printer ink cartridges are secretly programmed to expire on a certain date.the unnamed woman from georgia says that a chip inside the cartridge tells the printer that it needs re filling even when it does not.the smart chip is dually engineered to prematurely register ink depletion and to render a cartridge unusable through the use of a built in expiration date that is not revealed to the consumer, the suit said.the price of inkjet printers has come down to as little as 34 but it could cost up to 1,700 in running costs over an 18 month period due to cartridge, a study by computeractive magazine revealed last year.
