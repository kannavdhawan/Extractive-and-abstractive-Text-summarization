the new strains of the cabir mobile phone virus use short range radio technology to leap to any vulnerable phone as soon as it is in range.the cabir virus only affects high end handsets running the symbian series 60 phone operating system.despite the warnings, there are so far no reports of any phones being infected by the new variants of cabir.however, the new cabir strains have this mistake corrected and will spread via short range bluetooth technology to any vulnerable phone in range.the risk of being infected by cabir is low because users must give the malicious program permission to download on to their handset and then must manually install it.finnish security firm f secure issued a warning about the new strains of cabir but said that the viruses do not do any damage to a phone.
