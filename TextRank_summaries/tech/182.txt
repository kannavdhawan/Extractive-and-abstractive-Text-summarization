mobile gig aims to rock 3g forget about going to a crowded bar to enjoy a gig by the latest darlings of the music press.now you could also be at a live gig on your mobile, via the latest third generation 3g video phones.3g technology lets people take, watch and send video clips on their phones, as well as swap data much faster than with 2g networks like gsm.people with 3g phones in the uk can already download football and music clips on their handsets.once you have paid, you can come and go as much as you like, because we expect the customers to be mobile, said 3 spokesperson belinda henderson.3 is looking to music as a way of persuading more people to take up the latest video phones.
