loyalty cards idea for tv addicts viewers could soon be rewarded for watching tv as loyalty cards come to a screen near you.any household hooked up to sky could soon be using smartcards in conjunction with their set top boxes.broadcasters such as sky and itv could offer viewers loyalty points in return for watching a particular channel or programme.sky will activate a spare slot on set top boxes in january, marketing magazine new media age reported.parents could pre pay for some content, as a kind of tv pocket money card, said nigel whalley, managing director of media consultancy decipher.viewers could even be rewarded for watching ad breaks, with ideas such as ad bingo being touted by firms keen to make money out of the new market, said mr whalley.for the bbc, revenue generating activity will be of little interest but the new development may prompt changes to freeview set top boxes, said mr whalley.
