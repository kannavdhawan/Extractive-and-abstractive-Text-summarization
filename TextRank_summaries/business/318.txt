mystery surrounds new yukos owner the fate of russia's yuganskneftegas the oil firm sold to a little known buyer on sunday is the subject of frantic speculation in moscow.russia's newspapers claimed that baikal was a front for gas monopoly gazprom, which had been expected to win.the sale has destroyed yukos, once the owner of yuganskneftegas, said founder mikhail khodorkovsky.the authorities have made themselves a wonderful christmas present russia's most efficient oil company has been destroyed, the interfax news agency quoted mr khodorkovsky as saying via his lawyers.gazprom had been expected to win the auction but is thought to have failed to get finance for the deal after a us court injunction barred it from taking part.they screwed up the financing, said ronald smith, an analyst at renaissance capital in moscow.the sale has however bought time for gazprom to raise the money needed for the purchase, analysts said.one scenario is that baikal will not pay when it is supposed to in two weeks time, putting yuganskneftegas back in the hands of bailiffs and back within the reach of gazprom.
