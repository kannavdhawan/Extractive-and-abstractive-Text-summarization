umbro profits lifted by euro 2004 uk sportswear firm umbro has posted a 222% rise in annual profit after sales of replica england football kits were boosted by the euro 2004 tournament.umbro, which recently lost sponsorship deals with chelsea and celtic, said on thursday it had signed a new four year agreement with scottish club rangers.it hopes 2005 sales will benefit from the launch of a new england replica shirt ahead of the 2006 world cup.in january, umbro announced its sponsorship agreement with chelsea, which gave umbro the lucrative right to make replica shirts, would end in 2006, five years earlier than expected.
