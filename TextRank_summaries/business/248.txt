survey confirms property slowdown government figures have confirmed a widely reported slowdown of the uk's housing market in late 2004.house prices were 11.8% higher on the year in the last quarter of 2004, down from 16.3% in the july to september quarter, the land registry said.the average house price in england and wales was 182,920, down from 187,971 in july september.the volume of sales between october and december dropped by nearly a quarter from the same period in 2003.in the capital, the volume of sales fell by 23% from 36,185 in 2003 to 28,041 for the same period in 2004.after registering falls at the back end of 2004, halifax said house prices rose by 0.8% in january and nationwide reported a rise of 0.4% in the first month of the year.
