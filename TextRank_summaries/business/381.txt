oil rebounds from weather effect oil prices recovered in asian trade on tuesday, after falling in new york on milder winter weather across the us.with winter temperatures staying relatively high in the northern us, a barrel of light crude ended monday down 1.33 to 42.12.with milder temperatures expected to continue in the northern parts of the us over the next few days at least, analysts have said the price of oil may fall further even if the decline was only temporary.despite a fall of more than 12 a barrel from the record highs reached in late october, the price of crude oil remains almost 30% higher than year ago levels.
