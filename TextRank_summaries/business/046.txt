industrial output falls in japan japanese industrial output fell in october while unemployment rose, casting further doubt on the strength of the country's economic recovery.production dropped 1.6% in october, reflecting a decline in exports, while unemployment levels edged up 0.1% to 4.7%, slightly higher than forecast.the economy has grown for six quarters but growth slowed dramatically in the last quarter amid weaker global demand.according to figures from the ministry of economy, trade and industry meti , the decline was led by a fall in demand for electronic parts for mobile phones and digital televisions.the outlook for november is positive so i don't think one can say that conditions have worsened just because of the fall in october, said a meti official.despite the rise in unemployment, jobless figures are still some way below historical highs of recent years.
