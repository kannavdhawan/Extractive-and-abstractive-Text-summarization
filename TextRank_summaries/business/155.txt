macy's owner buys rival for 11bn us retail giant federated department stores is to buy rival may department stores for 11bn 5.7bn .the deal will bring together famous stores like macy's, bloomingdale's and marshall field's, creating the largest department store chain in the us.the two companies, facing competition from the likes of wal mart, tried to merge two years ago but talks failed.may has struggled to compete against larger department store groups such as federated and other retailers such as wal mart.we have taken the first step toward combining two of the best department store companies in america, creating a new retail company with truly national scope and presence, said terry lundgren, federated's chairman.
