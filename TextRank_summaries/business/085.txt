home loan approvals rising again the number of mortgages approved in the uk has risen for the first time since may last year, according to lending figures from the bank of england.new loans in december rose to 83,000, slightly higher than november's nine year low of 77,000.mortgage lending rose by 7.1bn in december, up from a 6.4bn rise in november.the modest rise in mortgage approvals and lending in december reinforces the impression that the housing market is currently slowing steadily rather than sharply, said global insight analyst howard archer, commenting on the boe's figures.
