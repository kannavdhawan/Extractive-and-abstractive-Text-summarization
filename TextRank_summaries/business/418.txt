us firm 'bids for lacroix label' a us firm has said it is in final negotiations with luxury goods group lvmh to buy the loss making christian lacroix haute couture house.privately held falic group bought two cosmetics brands, hard candy and urban decay, from lvmh in early 2003.but one of the three brothers behind the falic group said the firm had also held talks with the designer christian lacroix, and wished to retain him.
