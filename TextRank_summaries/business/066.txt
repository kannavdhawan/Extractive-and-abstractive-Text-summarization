german business confidence slides german business confidence fell in february knocking hopes of a speedy recovery in europe's largest economy.munich based research institute ifo said that its confidence index fell to 95.5 in february from 97.5 in january, its first decline in three months.we're surprised that the ifo index has taken such a knock, said dz bank economist bernd weidensteiner.economy and labour minister wolfgang clement called the dip in february's ifo confidence figure a very mild decline .he said that despite the retreat, the index remained at a relatively high level and that he expected a modest economic upswing to continue.analysts said that the ifo figures and germany's continuing problems may delay an interest rate rise by the european central bank.
