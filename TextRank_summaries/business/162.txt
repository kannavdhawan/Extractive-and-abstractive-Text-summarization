verizon 'seals takeover of mci' verizon has won a takeover battle for us phone firm mci with a bid worth 6.8bn 3.6bn , reports say.the two firms are expected to seal the deal on monday morning, according to news agency reports, despite what was thought to be a higher bid from qwest.the us telecoms market is consolidating fast, with former long distance giant at t being bought by former subsidiary sbc earlier this year for 16bn.mci is the us's second biggest long distance firm after at t.a merger between mci and verizon would be the fifth billion dollar telecoms deal since october.buying mci would give either qwest or verizon access to mci's global network and business based subscribers.
