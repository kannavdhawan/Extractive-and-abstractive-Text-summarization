'post christmas lull' in lending uk mortgage lending showed a post christmas lull in january, indicating a slowing housing market, lenders have said.both the council of mortgage lenders cml and building society association bsa said lending was down sharply.the cml said gross mortgage lending stood at 17.9bn, compared with 21.8bn in january last year.the cml said lending for house purchases and gross mortgage lending were 29% and 18% lower year on year respectively.
