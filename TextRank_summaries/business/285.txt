eu 'too slow' on economic reforms most eu countries have failed to put in place policies aimed at making europe the world's most competitive economy by the end of the decade, a report says.the study, undertaken by the european commission, sought to assess how far the eu has moved towards meeting its economic targets.their economic targets became known as the lisbon agenda.but the commission report says that, in most eu countries, the pace of economic reform has been too slow, and fulfilling the lisbon ambitions will be difficult if not impossible.
