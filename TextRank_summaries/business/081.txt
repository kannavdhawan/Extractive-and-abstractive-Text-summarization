bmw to recall faulty diesel cars bmw is to recall all cars equipped with a faulty diesel fuel injection pump supplied by parts maker robert bosch.the faulty part does not represent a safety risk and the recall only affects pumps made in december and january.audi and mercedes benz were also supplied with the defective diesel fuel injection pumps but neither of them have to recall any vehicles.a spokesman for daimlerchrysler, parent company of mercedes benz, said it will however have to halt some production.audi said it had been hit by production bottlenecks, due to a shortage of unaffected bosch parts.
