ryanair in 4bn boeing plane deal budget airline ryanair has placed an order for 70 boeing 737 800 planes, in a deal valued at 4bn 2.1bn which should lead to 2,500 new ryanair jobs.ryanair said the new planes would help it to cut operating costs further.ryanair chairman david bonderman said that the 737 800 had significantly reduced our unit operating costs and allowed us to reduce air fares each year for the last five years .with this new order and new pricing in place, ryanair expects that unit operating costs excluding fuel will continue to fall each year for the next five years, he added.
