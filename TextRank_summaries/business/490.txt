brazil jobless rate hits new low brazil's unemployment rate fell to its lowest level in three years in december, according to the government.the brazilian institute for geography and statistics ibge said it fell to 9.6% in december from 10.6% in november and 10.9% in december 2003.ibge also said that average monthly salaries grew 1.9% in december 2004 from december 2003.however, average monthly wages fell 1.8% in december to 895.4 reais 332 179.3 from november.the jobless rate average for the whole of 2004 was 11.5%, down from 12.3% in 2003, the ibge said.
