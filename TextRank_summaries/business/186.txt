huge rush for jet airways shares indian airline jet airways' initial public offering was oversubscribed 16.2 times, bankers said on friday.jet airways, a low fare airline, was founded by london based ex travel agent naresh goya, and controls 45% of the indian domestic airline market.the price at which its shares will begin trading will be agreed over the weekend, bankers said.india began to open up its domestic airline market previously dominated by state run carrier indian airlines in the 1990s.
