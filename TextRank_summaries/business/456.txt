gazprom, the biggest gas company in the world, took over nuclear fuel giant atomstroieksport in october 2004.gazprom is one of the key players in the complex russian energy market, where the government of vladimir putin has made moves to regain state influence over the sector.gazprom is set to merge with state oil firm rosneft, the company that eventually acquired yuganskneftegas, the main unit of embattled oil giant yukos.
