ba to suspend two saudi services british airways is to halt its flights from london heathrow to jeddah and riyadh in saudi arabia from 27 march.ba currently operates four flights per week from heathrow to jeddah, and three weekly journeys to riyadh.the decision to suspend flights between the uk and saudi arabia is a difficult one to make as we have enjoyed a long history of flying between the two countries, said ba director of commercial planning, robert boyle.
