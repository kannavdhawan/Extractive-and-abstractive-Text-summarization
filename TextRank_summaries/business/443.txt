kraft cuts snack ads for children kraft plans to cut back on advertising of products like oreo cookies and sugary kool aid drinks as part of an effort to promote healthy eating.it means commercials for some of its most famous snacks and cereals shown during early morning cartoon shows on tv will now be replaced by food and drink qualifying for kraft's new sensible solution label.but the firm said it would continue to advertise all its products in media seen by parents and all family audiences.
