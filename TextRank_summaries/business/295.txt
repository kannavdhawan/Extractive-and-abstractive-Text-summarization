business confidence dips in japan business confidence among japanese manufacturers has weakened for the first time since march 2003, the quarterly tankan survey has found.december's confidence level was below that seen in september, the bank of japan said.it will feel a bit slower next year than this year, and growth may be a bit more gentle but the situation is that the recovery will continue, said economy minister heizo takenaka.in the bank of japan's december survey, the balance of big manufacturers saying business conditions are better, minus those saying they are worse, was 22, down from 26 in september.big manufacturers were even more pessimistic about the first quarter of 2005 their views suggest the march reading could go as low as 15 still in positive territory, but weaker.companies reported they expect to invest 7.7% more in the year to march 2005 than the previous year up from expectations of 6.1% increase in the september tankan.
